Paper Chair

$ 450.00 AUD

Indoor / outdoor chair

520mm wide x 430 deep x 740 tall

A character that plays with changing light, the Paper Chair is the result of our experiments with creating strength and support with folded plains. Both seat and backrest are folded to follow the contours of the body and offer multiple supportive plains for the sitter.

For outdoor protection from the elements, the frame, backrest and seat pan are electroplated in zinc prior to being finished in UV stable powder coat. The colours offered on our website are a base range, please enquire if you are interested in further options. To add a little interest, the Paper Chair may be colour coded by varying the colours of the seat pan and frame.

High density plastic feet are fitted as standard and are replaceable if required, they offer both floor protection and noise reduction

All Tuckbox products are manufactured locally at our Melbourne workshop, allowing us to oversee production and maintain a consistent high quality.

Please allow four weeks for fabrication.

Designed and made by Daniel & Anthony de Groot.
